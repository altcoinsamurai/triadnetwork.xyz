FAQ

Common questions.

Is it really free?

Yes. We make money when you make money — 30% of the campaigns we source. If you don't earn, we don't earn. No retainers, no signup fees, no hidden charges.

I only have 300 followers. Can I apply?

Absolutely. We care about content quality, niche depth, and consistency — not vanity counts. Half our pipeline is built specifically for the creators agencies refuse to talk to.

Do I have to take every campaign?

No. You choose what to promote. We'll never pressure you to shill something you don't believe in — protecting your audience is how we both win long-term.

What does the brand buildout include?

Custom PFP, X banner, optimised bio with CTA, content templates, and a complete colour palette. Multi-platform adaptation included. All assets are yours to keep, even if you leave the network.

How do payouts work?

A project funds the campaign. You deliver the content. We verify, then send your 70% to your preferred wallet or bank within 7 days. Triad keeps 30% for sourcing, contracts, and account management.

Can I leave the network anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-in periods, no exit fees. You keep every brand asset we built for you. The directory listing simply comes down.

What's the cohort size?

We're capping the founding cohort at 100 creators. After that, applications continue but the bar rises — we'd rather under-recruit than over-promise.

Do you take equity in my brand?

Never. Your audience, your brand, your IP — all yours. We only take a campaign-level revenue split. Nothing else.
