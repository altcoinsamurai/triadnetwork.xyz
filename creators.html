FAQ

Common Questions

Is it really free?

Yes. We make money when you make money — 30% of campaigns we source. If you don't earn, we don't earn.

I only have 300 followers. Can I apply?

Absolutely. We care about content quality and consistency, not vanity metrics.

Do I have to accept every campaign?

No. You choose what you promote. We'll never pressure you to shill something you don't believe in.

What does the brand buildout include?

Custom PFP, X banner, optimised bio, content templates, and a colour palette. Everything to look professional.

How do campaigns work?

A project needs creator distribution. We match them with you. You create, we handle contracts. 70% to you within 7 days.

Can I leave anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. You keep your brand assets.
