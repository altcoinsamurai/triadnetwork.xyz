Common Questions

Is it really free to join?

Yes. We make money when you make money — through the 30% we take on campaigns we source for you. If you don't earn, we don't earn. Fully aligned.

I only have 300 followers. Can I still apply?

Absolutely. We care about content quality and consistency, not vanity metrics. Some of the best crypto analysts have small followings. That's exactly who we want to amplify.

Do I have to accept every campaign?

No. You choose what you promote. We'll never pressure you to shill something you don't believe in. Your credibility is your most valuable asset — we protect it.

What does the brand buildout include?

Custom PFP design, X/Twitter banner, optimised bio copy, content templates (thread starters, visual post layouts), and a colour palette. Everything you need to look professional.

How do campaigns work?

A project in our network needs creator distribution. We match them with relevant creators from our directory. You receive a brief, create content, post it. Payment is processed through us — you receive 70% within 7 days of campaign completion.

Can I leave anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. You keep your brand assets, your profile comes down, and we part ways. Simple.
