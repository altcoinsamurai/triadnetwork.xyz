MAIV — Democratising Institutional-Grade Capital Markets

MAIV (Multi Asset Investment Vehicle) is a European-registered platform bridging DeFi with traditional finance, enabling broader access to secure, high-yield investment opportunities across real-world assets through tokenised financial agreements.

The Opportunity

Bridging a $400 trillion gap

Traditional capital markets manage over $400 trillion globally, yet access remains gated behind high minimums and complex structures. MAIV uses Web3 to fractionalise and democratise these opportunities without sacrificing compliance.

The Platform

How MAIV works

  • Real Estate Development Pools — fractionalised early-stage property projects, up to 20% annual yields
  • Commercial Lending — tokenised financial agreements with established business backing
  • Refinancing Solutions — improved terms for businesses, secure returns for investors
  • White Label Platform — organisations launch branded investment platforms on MAIV infrastructure
Token Utility

The $MAIV ecosystem

  • Governance — weighted voting on platform decisions and fee allocation
  • Staking — earn additional tokens with duration-weighted vote power
  • Launchpad Access — early seed-stage funding allocations via token lock
  • Platform Subscription — $MAIV as membership key to full product suite
  • Portfolio Access — gamified staking for luxury real estate stays and events
