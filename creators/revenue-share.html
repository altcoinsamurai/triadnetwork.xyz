The Model

Transparent. Simple. Fair.

Every campaign that flows through Triad Creators operates on a 70/30 split. You keep 70% of the campaign value. We keep 30% to cover deal sourcing, project vetting, contract management, and platform operations.

70% — You

Your creative work, your audience, your credibility. The majority of the value stays where it was created — with you.

30% — Triad

Deal sourcing, project vetting, contract management, payment processing, dispute resolution, and platform operations.

7-Day Payment

Campaign completed? You receive your 70% within 7 days. No 30-day invoice cycles, no "payment pending" limbo.

No Hidden Fees

No platform fees, no membership costs, no percentage cuts on top of the split. 70/30 is 70/30. Full stop.

Why 70/30

We looked at every model. This one's right.

Most KOL agencies pay creators flat fees — $50 for a tweet, $200 for a thread. That model is broken because it disconnects compensation from impact. A thread that drives 10,000 clicks pays the same as one that drives 10.

Our model is different. Campaign budgets are set by the project. You keep 70% of whatever that budget is. Bigger projects, bigger payouts. Better results, more repeat campaigns. Your growth directly increases your earning potential.

  • Traditional agency model: Creator gets $50-$200 flat fee. Agency keeps 80%+
  • KOL marketplace model: Creator sets rates, platform takes 15-25%. But you source your own deals
  • Triad model: We source the deals, vet the projects, handle logistics. You keep 70%. Best of both worlds
